1)	What does .append() method does? 

Lets us add items to the end of a list, increasing its size.

2)	What does .remove() method does? 

Lets us remove an item from the list.

3)	What does .extend() method does?

 The length of the list increases by number of elements in its argument.

4)	What is the difference between .pop() and .clear() method?

 The difference is that .clear will get rid of the entire list and .pop will get rid of last element of the list
